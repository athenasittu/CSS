Places:

Name Location About Rating
Washington Park Arboretum Seattle, Washington From fall to winter to spring to summer, there is never a wrong time to visit the arboretum. It has your classic beautiful fall leaves during the autumn months. It has your cherry blossoms during the spring. Come for a stroll, a run, a bike ride, or a simple excursion. 9/10
Discovery Park Magnolia, Seattle Do you want to go hiking, but don't wanna drive an hour away? Well, Discovery Park is the perfect place for you to go. With over 12 miles of trails for you to enjoy, this trail offers hikers a little bit of the forest, beach, and lighthouses. 9/10
Lake Union Park Westlake, Seattle On the south edge of Lake Union, this park is the perfect place for a family gathering, a date, and more. During sunny days, you can find people paddelboarding, jumping into the lake, and picnicking. Venture out to the streets and you can find all types of restaurants from Sizzle and Crunch to Tacos Chukis. 8/10
